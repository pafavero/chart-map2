Example of the integration leaflet map with d3 diagrams

Displaying tiny histograms over a map

The displayed data on the charts come from:
  • GDP per capita (current US$) from World bank - indicator NY.GDP.PCAP.CD
  • GINI index - 2013 (World Bank estimate) from World bank - indicator SI.POV.GINI This index measures the degree of inequality in the distribution of family income in a country.
  • CO2 emissions - 2011 (metric tons per capita) World bank - indicator EN.ATM.CO2E.PC Carbon dioxide emissions are those stemming from the burning of fossil fuels.

The data of background map has been extracted from the free source Natural Earth Data

The background map is generated via leafletjs

Additionally Js-libraries which I used:

  • d3.js
  • jquery
  • chroma
